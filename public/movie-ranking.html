The Best Movies According To Shayleen

My Top 3 Movies Of All-time

Fair Play

Highlights the volatility of the male ego in relationships. An eye-opener for all modern women as it addresses a silent enemy for all high-achieving women in relationship.

Grown-Ups (1&2)

The best comedy movie, in my opinion. Great actors. Puts forward the message of friendship and it's importance in life.

Anyone But You

A wonderfully romantic comedy. A great watch for any day.
